Grenadiers Football Club

History: The Grenadiers Football Club was established in Toronto in October 2013. The Club is composed of male athletes. The Club is part of the Haitian Youth Movement Canada which is an entity of the Sylvenie Lindor Foundation. The Haitian Youth Movement Canada is a place for youth to take action and develop leadership skills they need in whatever paths they chose to follow in life. The Grenadiers Football Club is sustained by the Sylvenie Lindor Foundation and the Haitian Youth Movement Canada
