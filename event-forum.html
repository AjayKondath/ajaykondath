Press Release

Toronto July 26th, 2016 - This year, the Sylvenie Lindor Foundation in partnership with Citizenship and Immigration Canada presents the second annual Multicultural Forum: Confederation- Climate Change & Cultural Diversity. For the very first time in Toronto, Jean Jean Roosevelt, the defensor of International Francophonie, the 2013 Gold Medal winner of Francophonie in the song category, and the artist with a thousand voices and talents, will be the featured entertainer.

What is the Multicultural Forum?

The Multicultural Forum: Confederation- Climate Change & Cultural Diversity is a cultural exchange and dialogue between youth 18 years and older from Haitian, Irakian, Syrian, Somalian, Jamaican, Cameroonian, and Congolese communities, and the public of North Etobicoke in a context of cultural integration in Canadian society. Aiming to create social cohesion, the Forum will help youth from these communities to explore the realities of the other.
